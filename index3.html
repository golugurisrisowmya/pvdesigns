About G. Padma

With over a decade of experience in the art of design, G. Padma has established herself as a skilled artisan specializing in both traditional Maggam embroidery work and modern computer-aided designs.

Her journey began with a passion for traditional Indian embroidery techniques, which she has perfected over the years. As technology evolved, so did her skills, expanding into digital design while maintaining the authentic essence of traditional craftsmanship.
